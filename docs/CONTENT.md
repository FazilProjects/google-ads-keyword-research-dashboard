# Content Guidelines

## Voice

The product voice should be clear, practical, and honest. It should sound like a junior performance marketer demonstrating a thoughtful planning workflow, not a platform making inflated automation claims.

## Required Messaging

Include a visible note that this is a local planning dashboard and portfolio project.

Recommended language:

- "Local planner only"
- "Portfolio project"
- "Not connected to Google Ads or keyword volume APIs"
- "Planning aid, not live keyword data"

## Avoid

- "Guaranteed results"
- "Official Google Ads data"
- "AI-powered campaign optimization" unless actual AI logic is added and clearly explained
- "Real-time CPC"
- "Live search volume"
- "Automated Google Ads campaign creation"
- Any claim that the tool launches or manages campaigns

## Content Sections

The dashboard should contain:

- Business brief input
- Business type selector
- Campaign goal selector
- Target location input
- Budget input
- Keyword research generator
- Search intent grouping
- Ad group structure planner
- Match type suggestions
- Negative keyword ideas
- Campaign structure preview
- Ad copy starter ideas
- Export keyword plan
- Reset functionality

## Example Business Brief

"A dental clinic offering teeth whitening, implants, and emergency appointments for patients in Lahore."

## Example Output Tone

Good:

"Use phrase and exact match for high-intent appointment searches."

Avoid:

"This keyword will deliver the best conversions."

Good:

"Negative keyword ideas can help reduce irrelevant research or job-seeking searches."

Avoid:

"These negatives will eliminate wasted spend."
